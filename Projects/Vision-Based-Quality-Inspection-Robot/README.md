# Design and Development of a Vision-Based Quality Inspection Robot

## Overview

This project involved the complete design and development of a vision-based quality inspection robot intended for automated manufacturing environments. The robot was developed following the New Product Development (NPD) process, from concept generation to detailed mechanical design, with a focus on manufacturability, modularity, and ease of assembly.

---

## Project Details

- **Year:** 2026
- **Software:** SolidWorks
- **Project Type:** Mechanical Design | Product Development | Automation

---

## Objectives

- Develop a robotic system for automated quality inspection.
- Design a modular mechanical structure for easy assembly and maintenance.
- Integrate an inspection module suitable for vision-based inspection.
- Generate complete engineering documentation for manufacturing.
